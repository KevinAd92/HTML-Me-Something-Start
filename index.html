Gateway to the West Arch

A river front national park with a national monument smack dab in the middle. St. Louis has a thing for doubling up on attractions. With the recenly rennovated underground museum, The St. Louis arch is an architectural masterpiece with a plethora of regional and country knowledge beneath its feet. The site of the yearly July 4th celebration and many other festivites. Constructed in 1965 the arch it strethes 630 feet making it the tallest accessible structue in Missouri and possibly the wester heimsphere.

Sports & Anheurser

Someone once said St. Louis is a drinking city with a baseball problem. Two of St. Louis more well known entities are the St. Louis Cardinals and Anheuser busch. They come together perfectly at Busch Stadium home of St. louis's baseball team. Formerly home to the NBA team the St. Louis Spirits & the NFLs St. Louis Rams. The city was in a bit of a hole when it comes to sports having only the MLB St. Louis Cardinals and St. Louis Blues of the NHL until recently where the city was able to acquire two new sports teams. THe XFL's Battlehawks have been a rising fan base where St.louis Edwards Jones dome(home to the hawks) was able to host the XFL Championship game. Then lastly Stil go the MLS St. Louis City FC team, who has inital success their first season but has since hit a sophomore slump the 2nd season around.
